WHAT CAN I DO?
Support Low-Carbon Airlines
The best way to reduce air-travel related carbon emissions is to not travel by plane in the first place. When air travel is necessary, you can evaluate ticket options based on carbon-impact and choose low carbon airlines.
View Airline Carbon Efficiency Ratings Research Sustainable Travel Options
Offset Your Carbon Emissions
Sometimes air travel is necessary. To reduce the impact of your travel on the planet, consider donating to carbon-reducing causes. Some organizations also allow you to directly offset the carbon footprint of a flight for less than $5.00.
Calculate Your Carbon Footprint Plant Some Trees Research Carbon-Offsetting Services
Take Action
Major Airlines and Corporations must take severe steps to hit climate targets, but are often slow to raise consumer sentiment. Use your voice to hold airlines responsible and demand their commitment to a long-term solution.
